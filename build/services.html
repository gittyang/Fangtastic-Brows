My Services

Microblading

The most natural form of permanent makeup. Microblading mimics fine hair which is why it looks so natural. This is perfect for those who want just a base for an everyday look with the option to enhance a dramatic look with makeup.

Time duration: 1 hour 30 mins - starting at $350

Cover up/ Correction - additional $100

Ombre/Powder Brows

A makeup look that gives you the “Instagram brow”. Ombre has a gradient where it goes lighter at the center of the eyebrow and gradually gets darker towards the center and ends of the brow; while powder is the same color throughout the brow.

Time duration: 3 hours - starting at $375

Cover up/ Correction - additional $100

Microshading

Combo brows give you the best of both worlds. You can have a natural look while having some shading throughout the brow. The center of the eyebrow has strokes while rest of the brow is shaded to look like your eyebrow pencil.

Time duration: 3 hours - starting at $400

Cover up/ Correction - additional $100

Touch-Up

Time duration: 1 hour 30 mins
