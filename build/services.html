My Services

Microblading

This technique involves the insertion of pigments into the skin to mimic fine hair which is why it looks so natural. This is perfect for those who want a base for an everyday look with the option to enhance the brows further with makeup.

Time duration: 1 hour 30 mins - starting at $350

Cover up/ Correction - additional $100

Ombre/Powder Brows

A brow technique that gives you the soft and powdery “Instagram brow”. Ombre has a gradient where it goes lighter at the center of the eyebrow and gradually gets darker towards the end of the eyebrow tip while powder brows has the same gradient throughout the brow.

Time duration: 3 hours - starting at $375

Cover up/ Correction - additional $100

Microshading

This method is a combination of hair strokes and shading to give you the best of both worlds. You can have a natural hair-textured look while also having some shading throughout the brow.

Time duration: 3 hours - starting at $400

Cover up/ Correction - additional $100

Touch-Up

Your second touch-up session is highly recommended to refine and detail your brows to address any other minor details that may be needed.

Time duration: 1 hour 30 mins

6 - 12 weeks: $100

3 - 12 weeks: $150

1 - 2 years: $250

2+ years: new set

Free consultation

Time duration: 30 mins - $0
